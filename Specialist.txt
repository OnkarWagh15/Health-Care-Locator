Cardiologist           - Specializes in diagnosing and treating diseases and conditions related to the heart and cardiovascular system.
Dermatologist          - Deals with the diagnosis and treatment of skin disorders, diseases, and conditions.
Gastroenterologist     - Focuses on disorders and diseases of the digestive system, including the stomach, intestines, liver, and pancreas.
Gynecologist           - Specializes in women's reproductive health, including the diagnosis and treatment of conditions related to female reproductive system.
Orthopedic             - Deals with the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal conditions, injuries, and disorders involving bones, muscles, and tendons.
Neurologist            - Specializes in disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.
Psychiatrist           - Focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of mental health disorders.
Pediatrician           - Specializes in the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents.
Ophthalmologist        - Deals with the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders and diseases, including vision problems and eye injuries.
Anesthesiologist       - Administers anesthesia and monitors patients' vital signs during surgical procedures or medical treatments.
Otolaryngologist       - Also known as an ear, nose, and throat (ENT) specialist, diagnoses and treats disorders related to ENT and related structures.
Urologist              - Focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of urinary tract disorders in both males and females.
Pulmonologist          - Specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of respiratory disorders, including lung diseases and conditions.
Oncologist             - Deals with the diagnosis and treatment of cancer.
Endocrinologist        - Focuses on disorders and conditions related to the endocrine system, which regulates hormones and metabolism.
Radiologist            - Interprets and analyzes medical images (X-Ray, MRI, CT-Scan) to aid in the diagnosis and treatment of various conditions.
Allergist              - Specializes in diagnosing and treating allergies and allergic reactions.
Nephrologist           - Focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of kidney diseases and conditions.
Optometrist            - Provides primary vision care, including eye examinations, prescribing corrective lenses, and detecting eye abnormalities.
Anatomical Pathologist - Examines tissue samples and performs autopsies to diagnose diseases and conditions.
Hematologist           - Specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders and conditions related to blood and blood-producing organs.
General Surgery        - Performs surgical procedures for a wide range of conditions and diseases.
Respirologist          - Deals with the diagnosis and treatment of respiratory disorders, particularly those affecting the lungs.
Neurosurgery           - Performs surgical procedures on the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves to treat various neurological conditions.
Dentist                - Focuses on oral health, including diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases and conditions affecting the teeth, gums, and mouth.
Pathologist            - Performs Lab Tests and diagnose reach results.
Sexologist             - Examines human sexual behaviour.
